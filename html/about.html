Hi, I'm Ana!
I am a 20 years old student, second year at the Faculty of Mathematics and Informatics, Bucharest, and I LOVE cooking.
I discovered my passion around the age of 14, while watching MasterChef. The dishes prepared were so fresh and rafined and I was amazed by the ingredients used in the making of them. So I started to recreate the recipies seen with the help of my mom. Who would've known today I would extend this passion to a fun business of catering?
